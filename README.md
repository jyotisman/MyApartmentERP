# MyApartmentERP
# Society ERP

A modern, open-source Apartment & Housing Society Management ERP built with Laravel 12, PHP 8.3, MySQL, and Bootstrap 5.

## Features

- Resident & Flat Management
- Maintenance Billing & Collection
- Auto Receipt Generation
- UTR Validation
- Income & Expense Management
- Visitor Parking
- Community Hall Booking
- Complaint Management
- Notice Board
- Meeting Minutes & Resolution Register
- Vendor & Agency Management
- Dashboard & Analytics
- PDF & Excel Reports
- Role-Based Access Control
- Audit Logs
- Responsive Design

## Technology Stack

- Laravel 12
- PHP 8.3
- MySQL 8
- Bootstrap 5
- Chart.js
- DomPDF
- Laravel Excel
